About

Barefoot Homes is a privately owned UK property development company. Our key focus remains on building exceptional residential properties in some of London and the South East’s most desirable areas. Underpinned by market knowledge and governed by integrity, Barefoot Homes is currently realising a number of potentially high-yield projects to add to our impressive portfolio.

Experience

The Directors of Barefoot Homes Limited possess over two decades of solid experience developing residential and commercial projects. They also boasts a track record of generating healthy return for investors.

London & South East

Barefoot Homes is solely focused on the UK’s most valuable real estate market: London and the South East. Our expertise encompasses all facets of property development, including sourcing prime sites, project management, architectural design, council approval, sales and marketing.

Partnerships

Barefoot Homes success is founded on the strength of our relationships. From key consultants to local councils, we have forged trusting, durable partnerships with a large number of investors.
